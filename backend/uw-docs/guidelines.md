# ClearPath Mortgage — Underwriting Guidelines

## Lender Overlays & Preferred Programs

### Preferred Conventional Lenders
- United Wholesale Mortgage (UWM): competitive pricing, fast turn times
- Rocket Pro TPO: strong pricing on jumbo
- loanDepot: good for self-employed with 2yr history

### Internal Overlays (Stricter than Agency)
- Minimum credit score: 640 (agency allows 620, but we require 640)
- Maximum DTI: 45% on conventional
- Investment properties: require 6-month reserves post-close
- Condos: require warrantable condo review; non-warrantable requires 25% down

## Income Documentation Matrix
| Employment Type  | Documentation Required                     |
|-----------------|---------------------------------------------|
| W-2 (full time) | 2yr W-2s + most recent paystub             |
| Self-employed   | 2yr personal + business tax returns, P&L   |
| 1099 contractor | 2yr 1099s + tax returns                    |
| Rental income   | Lease agreements + Schedule E              |

## Rate Lock Policy
- Standard: 30-day lock (no cost)
- 45-day: +0.125% in price
- 60-day: +0.25% in price

## Recent Guideline Updates (2025)
- FHFA raised conforming limits to $806,500 baseline / $1,209,750 high-cost
- FHA MIP reduced to 0.55% annually for most 30yr loans
- SOFR-based ARMs now standard; LIBOR fully retired
